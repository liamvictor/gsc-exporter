How to Read the Key Performance Metrics Report

This report provides a monthly summary of your site's core Google Search Console metrics over the last 16 months.

Report Columns

month
The calendar month for the data row.
clicks
The total number of clicks from Google search results.
impressions
The total number of times your site appeared in search results.
ctr
Click-Through Rate (CTR), which is Clicks / Impressions. This is a key indicator of how compelling your search result snippets are.
position
The average ranking of your site in the search results for that month.

How to Use This Report

Use this report to track the high-level performance trends of your website. Look for:

  • Seasonal Trends: Identify if your traffic fluctuates at certain times of the year.
  • Impact of Work: See if there are significant changes in metrics after major site updates or SEO work.
  • Long-Term Growth: Track the overall trajectory of your clicks and impressions to measure long-term SEO success.
