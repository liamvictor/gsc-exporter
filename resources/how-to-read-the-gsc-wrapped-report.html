How to Read the Google Organic Wrapped Report

The Google Organic Wrapped report provides a fun, year-in-review summary of your website's Google Search Console performance. Here's what each section means.

Your Year in Numbers

This section gives you a high-level overview of your site's performance over the specified period.

  • Total Clicks: The total number of times a user clicked through to your site from a Google search results page.
  • Total Impressions: The total number of times a link to your site appeared in search results.
  • Unique Queries: The count of distinct search terms that showed your site in the results.
  • Unique Pages: The count of distinct pages on your site that appeared in search results.
Your Top Performers

This section highlights your star content and keywords.

  • Your #1 Page: The single page on your site that received the most clicks.
  • Your #1 Query: The single search query that drove the most clicks to your site.
  • Your Busiest Month: The calendar month in which you received the highest number of clicks.
Your GSC Story

This section provides human-readable sentences that summarize the key findings from the report, making the data easy to understand and share.
