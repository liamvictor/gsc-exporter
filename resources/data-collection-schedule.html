GSC Data Collection: Best Practice Schedule

This guide outlines a recommended schedule for running the GSC data export scripts for a single domain. Following this routine will help you build a comprehensive historical dataset and stay on top of performance trends.

Daily

snapshot-report.py

Purpose: To get a quick, high-level overview of your site's performance from the previous day.

Why: This script is ideal for daily checks to catch any immediate and significant changes, such as sharp drops in traffic or clicks, that might indicate technical issues or ranking penalties.

Weekly

performance-analysis.py

Purpose: To compare performance between the last 7 days and the preceding 7 days.

Why: A weekly analysis helps you understand short-term trends. It's useful for measuring the initial impact of SEO changes, content updates, or the effect of seasonality on your organic traffic.

Monthly

key-performance-metrics.py

Purpose: To track the most important high-level metrics (Clicks, Impressions, CTR, Position) on a month-by-month basis.

Why: This forms the basis of your monthly performance reporting. It smooths out daily fluctuations and provides a clear picture of your site's long-term growth trajectory.

gsc-pages-queries.py

Purpose: To generate a detailed report connecting search queries to the specific pages they drive traffic to.

Why: This is crucial for content strategy. A monthly run helps you identify which pages are performing well, discover new keyword opportunities, and find pages that may need optimization for better query alignment.

query-position-analysis.py

Purpose: To analyze the distribution of your keyword rankings (e.g., how many keywords are in positions 1-3, 4-10, etc.).

Why: This provides a more nuanced view of your SEO performance than average position alone. It helps you see if you are gaining more top-ranking keywords or if keywords are slipping to lower positions, guiding your optimization efforts.

Quarterly

gsc_pages_exporter.py

Purpose: To export a full list of all URLs from your site that have appeared in Google Search over the last 90 days.

Why: A quarterly export is great for a comprehensive content audit. You can identify orphaned pages, discover URLs with impressions but no clicks, and get a complete picture of your site's search footprint for strategic review.
